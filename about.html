📌 About Me!

Hello there! My name is Karim Emad, and I'm thrilled to share my journey with you.

I have a bachelor's degree in computer systems engineering. During my time at university, I discovered my interest in graphic design and began learning the craft. I enrolled in various courses to hone my skills and gain practical experience.

After graduation, I realized that I wanted to combine my programming skills with my love of design to create intuitive and visually engaging user interfaces. This led me to pursue a career in UI/UX design.

To prepare for this transition, I enrolled in various courses, including the UX Nanodegree program on Udacity and the Google UX Path on Coursera. I also joined the ITI UX camp to gain practical experience. In addition, I worked on the freelancer platform to build my portfolio and gain exposure to different industries, such as e-commerce, education, and fashion. Through this experience, I practiced UX processes and improved my skills through client feedback and iteration.

I have been working as a UI/UX designer for more than two years. I am passionate about using my skills to solve real-world problems and create user-centric designs that make a positive impact. I am excited to continue learning and growing in my career as a designer.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and my journey. I can't wait to see what the future holds!
