📖 My Story so far

Hello there! My name is Karim Emad, and I'm thrilled to share my journey with you.

I am a Computer Systems Engineering graduate from Zagazig University. During my time in university, I discovered my passion for graphic design, and I actively participated in student activities to enhance my skills in this area.

After graduation, I decided to explore software development to expand my skill set and enhance my technical knowledge. While I enjoyed learning full-stack and front-end programming, I missed the creative aspect of graphic design. I realized that I wanted to combine my programming skills with my love of design to create intuitive and visually engaging user interfaces. This led me down the path of UI/UX design.

To prepare for this new career direction, I enrolled in the UX program on Udacity and the Google UX path on Coursera. These courses helped me develop a deeper understanding of the principles and practices of UI/UX design. I was fascinated by the science behind user behavior and the art of designing digital experiences that are both aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly.

To gain practical experience, I joined ITI and started freelancing on platforms like Freelancer. I worked on UI projects and gradually transitioned to UX research, where I was able to apply my technical skills to real-world problems. The more I immersed myself in the UX process, the more I realized how much I enjoyed it.

I am excited to continue growing in the UI/UX design field, incorporating my creativity and technical expertise to create innovative and user-friendly solutions.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and my journey. I can't wait to see what the future holds!
